Computer Science and Engineering(CSE)Department

Welcome to the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department at our university. Our department is dedicated to providing high-quality education and research in the field of computer science and engineering.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computer science and engineering education and research, fostering innovation and technological advancements.

Key Features of Our Department:

  • State-of-the-art laboratories equipped with the latest technology.
  • Experienced and dedicated faculty members.
  • Wide range of research areas including artificial intelligence, data science, software engineering, and more.
  • Active participation in industry collaborations and internships.
  • Engaging workshops, seminars, and hackathons for students.

Programs Offered:

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (B.Tech CSE)
  • Master of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (M.Tech CSE)
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering
