Hanging Bridge

This bridge was built during 1984 or around that time. In Bangla this is known as Jhulonto bridge (which means Hanging bridge in English) although this is not a true hanging bridge. It has two pillars at both sides, but inside water. It was rainy season, and not that many tourists available here. I came to know that it would be much crowded during the winter as that is the peak tourist time. Also, during some rainy season this bridge sometimes goes under water as the water level rises.
